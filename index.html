Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a suit take to make?

Typically, a bespoke suit takes 3 to 4 weeks. This includes multiple fittings to ensure the perfect fit.

Do you offer rush services?

Yes, we offer rush services for alterations and urgent repairs. Please call us to check availability.

What forms of payment do you accept?
